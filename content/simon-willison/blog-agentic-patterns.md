## 3. Testing and QA
Methodologies including test-driven development, executing tests first, and manual quality assurance.

## 4. Understanding code
Techniques for explaining codebases through walkthroughs and interactive demonstrations.

## 6. Appendix
Reusable prompt templates for various tasks.
