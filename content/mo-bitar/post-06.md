# AI broke the one thing we can't fix
**Date:** March 24, 2026

*Note: This article is behind a paywall. Only the introduction was accessible.*

The article begins with a reference to Nikita Bier, Head of Product at X, who posted a warning about an impending communications crisis. According to Bier's statement, within ninety days, messaging platforms including iMessage, phone calls, and Gmail will become inundated with spam and automated content to the point of becoming unusable.

Notably, Bier emphasized a critical problem: "we will have no way to stop it."

The author notes that Bier's warning carries weight precisely because he holds a significant position at a major technology platform, rather than being speculation from an anonymous internet user. The article addresses how artificial intelligence capabilities have created a situation where traditional communication channels face an existential threat that current technical and organizational measures cannot adequately counter.
